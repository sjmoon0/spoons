Objective of game: Get a spoon

A spoon can be grabbed when you have 4 of a kind in your hand(top four cards on your game screen), or when another player grabs a spoon.

When the right side of the screen is highlighted in pink, you are able to draw a card. To draw this card, swipe the card left.

If you want to keep the card you drew, swipe up on a card in your hand to replace it with the current card.

Once you collect 4 of a kind or another player grabs a spoon, you can grab a spoon!
